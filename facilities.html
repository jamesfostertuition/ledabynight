Facilities

Lessons can be taken either via home visits, or using my personal teaching studio, situated in Newfoundpool, Leicester.

  • Variety of guitars and instruments from brands including: Fender, Raimundo, Squire, Ibanez and Vintage
  • Amplifiers from Orange, Marshall, Blackstar and Blackheart
  • Variety of effects from brands including: Boss, Marshall, Electro-Harmonix, Digitech, Zvex and Ashdown
  • Recording and playback capability through Logic Pro using Focusrite Preamps, Shure and Rode Microphones
  • Student violins in half and full sizes
  • Student nylon string guitar in half size
