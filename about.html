About Me

During my teenage years I developed a passion for music; I learnt guitar, played in bands and performed. Since then my interest in music has grown, and continues to influence my studies and career path.

As a primary school teacher of 5 years, I have an excellent understanding of various methods of teaching, and the importance of adapting to a student’s style of learning in order to ensure progression.

I am a member of the Musicians’ Union, complete regular updates on child protection and safeguarding with North West Leicestershire County Council, and have an enhanced Disclosure and Barring Service (formally CRB) check that is available to see upon request.

Qualifications:

  • Postgraduate Certificate in Education (PGCE) Primary Specialisation
  • Qualified Teacher Status (QTS) England and Wales
  • BSc Sound Engineering and Design (Hons)
  • RSL Electric Guitar Grade 8
