About

Hi I’m Meg, the solo lady behind Leda by Night lingerie. I set up my little business three years ago, in order to inject some much needed fun and creativity into my life!

My aim is to create lingerie that makes the wearer feel amazing. I keep comfort and wearability as well as style in mind, and many of my designs are inspired by my own loves and interests (whether that’s the magical feeling of a chill Autumn evening or my favourite space themed video games!)

Everything at Leda is designed, sewn and packed in my little studio at Makers Yard, Leicester.

Let's talk! Feel free to get in touch with me via the contact page.
