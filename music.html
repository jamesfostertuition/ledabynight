      Beginners

      Firm foundations are the basis of a versatile musician. During the beginning stages it is important to focus on body position, holding the instrument and production of sound. Without this, the learner will make progress at first, but this will be hindered as they move onto more complex pieces of music or more complicated techniques.

      Learners will work on a custom syllabus that develops a versatile musical knowledge as well as a working knowledge of music theory. Lessons are adadpted during lessons to cater for different learners.
