Schools

Individual Instrument Tuition

I believe that music in schools should be kept alive through passionate teaching of a high standard.

Teachers

All lessons are taught by myself. I am a qualified teacher with QTS (Primary Specialisation), 6 years experience of working with schools and all relevant safeguarding information. Upon arrival, the school are provided with a valid, up to date DBS check, certificate of public liability insurance and all documentation with regards to students.

My requirements

  • Children with a passion for music and a commitment to learning
  • A suitable room to accommodate lessons

Syllabus

Traditional techniques and music theory are implemented and taught in a way that works with present day music. Students learn from a carefully crafted syllabus to take beginners to a Grade 1 standard within 3 terms of lessons with consistent attendance and practise.
Both guitar and violin are taught to the Trinity Guildhall classical syllabus although other exam boards can be used upon request of the parents/school.

Administration and Invoicing

Enrolment forms, invoicing and collection of fees for music lessons can be time consuming and a strain on an already busy School Office. I provide all administration with regards to new students, invoicing and collecting payment directly from parents.
